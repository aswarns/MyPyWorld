Title: Flatmate Bill
Description: An app that gets as input of a bill for a particular period
and the days that eah of the flatmates stayed in the house for that period
and returns how much each flatmate has to pay. It also generates a PDF
report stating the names of the flatmates, and how much each of them had to pay.
Objects: Bill:
               Amount
               Period
         Flatmate:
            name
            day_in_house
            pays(bill)
         pdfReport:
            filename
            generate(flatmate1, flatmate2, bill)
